Rhythm is the quiet pulse beneath everything we do—the turning of seasons, the lessons of the land, and the meaning found in the work of tending a life. Here we follow the wheel of the year, honor the shifts in light and weather, and reflect on how the land shapes us as much as we shape it.

Seasonal Reflections & Field Notes

This space will grow into a journal of the land: solstice and equinox notes, seasonal markers, small observations, and the stories that emerge from paying attention. Over time, it will become a record of how this place changes—and how we change with it.
